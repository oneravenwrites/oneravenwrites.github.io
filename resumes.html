My name is Kim Hálás, and I'm oneRaven.
Having spent a majority of my life hunting for my passion, I have accumulated a breadth of skills seldom found in technical fields. On the other hand, my technical skills are also seldom found in the more creative fields of design. Despite the endeavor, there has always been an underlying desire and aptitude for writing, regardless of the topic or style.

I created oneRaven as the synthesis of all of the knowledge I have acquired over the past couple of decades. I started out my journey wanting to follow in my father's footsteps in real estate. I obtained the credentials after two years of business and real estate classes. During that time, I also had my first formal introduction to graphic design and video production working as an assistant at a small firm. I wrote copy for high-end listings for real estate professionals nationwide, and produced "videos" from the still photographs they would send us.
Most of my spare time is spent rockhounding in our vast expanses and hills, or tinkering in the garage and welding. I also dabble in photography; mostly landscapes and black/white still life.

Let me show you what versatility and a different perspective can bring to your project.
