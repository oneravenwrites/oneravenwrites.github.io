oneRaven works

Let's get you back to work. My process is simple: I offer a free initial consultation, in-person or virtually, so we can talk about the best plan of attack to land the job or career you want. From there, I'll start working on your resumé and either provide brand new, never-before-seen, sparkling copies to start sending out, or send your copy back with my edits and suggestions for you to edit and modify as you wish.
Keep reading to see what option would best suit your situation.



Total Overhaul

Your resumé needs some TLC. Like big time. If you want, I can produce a couple different variants based on the kinds of jobs you're looking for. And they won't be your standard, boring everyday resumés. Fancy, targeted cover letters can be included. Turn-around is generally less than 5 business days.

Editing/Proofreading

So you're pretty happy with what you have, but it needs some polish. Send it over and I'll be happy to give it some shine and make some suggestions. Turn-around is generally less than 48 hours.

Job Coaching & Interviews

I can't interview for you, but I can teach you how to land more interviews and 'wow' your new boss. Meetings are generally done virtually, and request that you schedule at least 48 hours in advance.
